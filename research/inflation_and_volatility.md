## What this category is

Inflation hedges are assets that **tend to hold value or benefit when inflation is higher than expected**.

Important point:

> Inflation hedges are defined by **behavior**, not by issuer.

Some are government-issued, some are real assets, some are financial hybrids.

You only need **two** here.

---

## A) **Inflation-linked government bonds (TIPS)**

### What this represents

* Direct protection against CPI inflation
* Government-backed
* Real (inflation-adjusted) yields

### How it works

* Principal increases when inflation rises
* Coupons are paid on the adjusted principal
* You are protected from *unexpected* inflation
